System Features

GCIT ATA

QR code scanning:

The system have the ability to scan QR codes attached to assets to retrieve information about them.

Asset tracking

The system should track the movement and location of assets, enabling you to know the current location of each asset

Asset identification

The system can be able to uniquely identify each asset using a QR code, providing asset details such as asset name, owner, and location.

Mobile access

The system allows you to access asset information using mobile devices, enabling you to manage your assets remotely.

Email Alerts

The system should be able to send email alerts to finance team to notify them when a faulty asset has been identified.

Real-time Data Syncing

The system ensures that any changes made in one system are immediately reflected in the other system.

Asset management is the process of identifying what you have, where it is, how much it's worth, and what condition it's in. It's about understanding the full lifecycle of your assets, from acquisition to disposal, and making strategic decisions to ensure that they are used effectively and efficiently.
