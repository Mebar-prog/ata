GCIT, Asset tagging application was built is to provide systematic and efficient ways to keep track of and manage physical assets

The college has a diverse range of assets, including computers, projectors, laboratory equipment, furniture, and other fixed assets. However, managing and tracking these assets is a challenging and time-consuming task that requires a manual inventory process. To address this challenge, GCIT college came up with the implementation of an asset tagging application. The asset tagging application will allow college administrators to assign unique identification numbers or barcodes to each asset, enabling real-time tracking and management of assets.

  • To scan the assets to trace out the owner.
  • To scan the assets to trace out the location.
  • To keep a record of the assets by generating QR codes for individual assets.
  • To report if there is any damage or loss of assets.
